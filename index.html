About Us

At BikeWorld, we've been transforming the cycling experience since 2015. What started as a dream in a small Seattle workshop has evolved into an innovative force in modern cycling, driven by our passion for exceptional design and sustainable transportation.

Features & Benefits:

Our ultralight carbon fiber frame combines with electronic 12-speed shifting for unmatched performance. Advanced features include GPS navigation, integrated LED lights, and Bluetooth connectivity. With hydraulic disc brakes and adjustable geometry, you'll experience superior control and comfort whether commuting or trail riding.

Specifications:
